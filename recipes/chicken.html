Chicken Alfredo

Description

This creamy pasta dish is made with fettuccine, chicken, cream, and Parmesan cheese. It's a classic Italian-American dish that's sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and add the cream, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to combine with the sauce. If the pasta seems dry, add a little reserved pasta water until desired consistency is reached.
  6. Serve immediately, topped with extra Parmesan cheese and black pepper if desired.
