Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This classic Italian dish is made with spaghetti, bacon, eggs, and cheese. It's a quick and easy meal that's perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 8 ounces bacon, chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a large skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove from skillet and drain on paper towels.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, black pepper, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the bacon and toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and pour in the egg mixture, tossing quickly to coat the pasta. The heat of the pasta will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce.
  6. If the pasta seems dry, add a little reserved pasta water until desired consistency is reached.
  7. Serve immediately, topped with extra Parmesan cheese and black pepper if desired.
