Vegetable Stir-Fry

Description

This healthy and colorful stir-fry is made with a variety of fresh vegetables and a flavorful sauce. It's a quick and easy meal that's perfect for a busy weeknight.

Ingredients

  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, cornstarch, garlic, and ginger. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp.
  3. Add the sauce to the skillet and stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the vegetables.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately, over rice or noodles if desired.
